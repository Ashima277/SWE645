About Me

Hello everyone, I am Ashima Sharma and I'm currently pursuing my MS in Software Engineering at George Mason University. I am passionate about full-stack development, cloud computing, and usability engineering. I am always eager to solve complex problems and build impactful software solutions. I have prior experience working as a full-stack developer at Eninrac Consulting Private Limited in India, where I led the development and implementation of user interface components, ensuring technical feasibility and optimizing performance. I also have research experience from my Master of Technology in Computer Science, where I had the opportunity to work on a government-funded project called AgroVaid. It was an advanced agricultural system integrating ARM controllers, 3D model generation, GSM, and audio/text input methods. The system allowed farmers to query crop and pest data via image recognition, audio, or text input, generating real-time results on a screen or through audio. It also used a 3D scanner to identify pests and match them with a database for detailed information. Outside of work, I like cooking for friends and family, playing badminton, and diving into a good book. These activities help me relax and recharge, offering a healthy balance to my busy schedule.
